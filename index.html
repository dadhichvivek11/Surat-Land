NRI services

Buy or sell land here, without leaving where you are.

We handle the process on the ground in Surat while you stay in your own country. That includes preparing documents, completing the legal process, arranging Power of Attorney, and following through to payment.

  • Document preparation, reviewed and shared for your sign-off
  • Power of Attorney arrangement for on-ground execution
  • Legal process managed and reported to you at each stage
  • Payment handled and confirmed to close the cycle

Title clearance

Carrying an old dispute on your land? We clear it.

Many landowners in this region hold parcels with unclear succession, old disputes, or pending mutation entries. We work through the revenue record and legal history to resolve these, so the land is ready to sell, develop, or hand down.

  • Review of revenue record and past ownership history
  • Resolution of pending disputes or mutation entries
  • A clean, sale-ready title at the end of the process
